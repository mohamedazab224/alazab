Intelligent Safety Features

NEX Media Wall is equipped with advanced safety sensors for secure and dependable operation. The anti-pinch sensor prevents the wall from closing if it encounters an obstacle, while the flame-on sensor ensures the fireplace area remains open when the fire is active. A temperature sensor continuously monitors conditions and provides audible alerts for any irregularities. These features offer peace of mind while giving you, as a designer, the assurance that your projects meet the highest standards.
